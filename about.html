I'm Surkhay Fatullayev, a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology student from Baku, Azerbaijan. I'm driven by curiosity about how systems — digital and human — connect and function.

My interest in technology started with games. Trying to understand how they worked led me down a rabbit hole of programming, systems design, and problem solving. Today that curiosity shapes how I approach every project I work on.

Through my coursework I've gained hands-on experience with web development, version control, and information systems principles. I enjoy translating ideas into working products — whether that's a webpage, a program, or an automated workflow.
